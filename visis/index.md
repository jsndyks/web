#### Sunday 25 Jan 2026 : 8pm, 

I spent a fair amount of time working on [IEEE VIS](https://ieeevis.org/) committees and organization for a decade or so from about 2012.

I was on the _Executive_ and _Steering Committees_ for a while and acively participated in the [the original reVISe activity](https://www.ieeevis.org/year/2026/blog/vis-2026-revise)

Welcome [Reflections on (part of) VIS](https://ieeevis.org/year/2026/blog/2025-11-11-vis-2025-OPC-blog/).

The [WebSite](https://ieeevis.org/) describes _VIS_ as ...

> "The premier forum for advances in visualization and visual analytics"

The [IEEE VIS Charter](https://ieeevis.b-cdn.net/vis_2025/2025-06-18%20VIS%20Charter%20-%20Amendment%20-%20Approved.pdf) tells us that ...

> "The objective of IEEE VIS is to provide the premier international forum for presenting innovations in visualization and visual analytics methods and applications across science, engineering, medicine, humanities, commerce, the arts and other areas."

That seems a bit lame to me. "_Presenting_"? To whom? For what?<br/>
These feel like important issues.


Let’s call them “_VIS IS…_” statements: 

**QUALITY** - _VIS IS_ the focus for the World’s best research in visualization, a beacon for the discipline that advances and promotes our knowledge and use of graphical approaches to problem solving;

**ACCESSIBILITY** - _VIS IS_ available to all as an inclusive opportunity for real-time engagement with and deep intellectual discourse about research that tests, improves and celebrates research;

**DIVERSITY** - _VIS IS_ inclusive of the diverse perspectives, methodologies, domains, epistemologies, participants and applications that are required for reliable research; 

**SUSTAINABILITY** - _VIS IS_ sustainable - minimising energy used, waste produced and damage incurred, while promoting innovative approaches to reducing the footprint of the conference;
 
**COMMUNITY** - _VIS IS_ a forum for research to engage with business, government, third sector organizations and both local and global communities to establish and share knowledge in ways that are mutually beneficial; 

**HUMANITY** - _VIS IS_ fair, treats people with respect and gives all those involved with the conference the time and resources to live fulfilling lives that while enhanced by research are nourished by plentiful rest, recreation and recuperation;
